Key Milestones in the History of Computing

The history of computing is marked by several key milestones:

  • 1822: Charles Babbage conceives the idea of the mechanical computer with his Difference Engine.
  • 1936: Alan Turing proposes the concept of a universal machine that could perform computations similar to a modern computer.
  • 1976: Steve Wozniak and Steve Jobs release the Apple I, one of the first personal computers.
  • 1990: Tim Berners-Lee invents the World Wide Web, revolutionizing the way information is accessed and shared.
